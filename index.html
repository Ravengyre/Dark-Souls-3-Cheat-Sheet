Cemetery of Ash

  • Head forward and follow the path to the right to get a Soul of a Deserted Corpse.
  • Return to the main path and continue forward to obtain thge Ashen Estus Flask.
  • Turn right and follow the narrow path to find a Ravenous Crystal Lizard, kill it to get a Titanite Scale. Pick up a Soul of an Unknown Traveler in the area.
  • Return to the main path and continue to the cliffside. Head left to the bonfire, gaining the Rest gesture.
  • Kill the hollows and follow the path to the right alongside the cliffside. Grab the five Firebombs at the end of the path.
  • Backtrack up the path to the message teaching you how to jump. Jump here onto the coffin to get a Titanite Shard.
  • Move through the archway ahead to fight the first boss, Iudex Gundyr. Defeat him to get the Coiled Sword.
  • Open the large double doors and move forward to pick up the Broken Straight Sword near some graves.
  • Follow along the cliffside past a couple of hollows. Grab the Homeward Bone.
  • Return to the main path and climb the hill. on the right you will have to fight an undead dog guarding an Ember.
  • Further up the hill along the left side, climb the stairs. Grab the East West Shield from the corpse in the tree.
  • Take the stairs on the right past the two hollows to get another Ember.
  • Or continue forward to fight Sword Master Saber. Killing him will net you the Uchigatana, Master's Attire, and Master's Gloves.
  • Return once more to the main path to enter Firelink Shrine.
